Classy Clay's Catering Progress Update

Catering Connect Solutions: Group 5

Project Manager Summary

This progress update documents the current direction of the Classy Clay's Catering prototype. The project is focused on creating a simple no-code/low-code system that supports request tracking, menu management, AI-assisted follow-up drafts, marketing content support, and repeat customer engagement.

The prototype is designed to support existing systems like Owner.com and Square instead of replacing them. Square will continue to handle final estimates, invoices, and payments. Our system focuses on the gaps identified during the client interview: better intake details, less back-and-forth communication, customer tracking, and marketing consistency.
